MHD Simulation

Magnetohydrodynamics of Earth's Magnetosphere

This simulation solves the MHD equations:

  • ∂ρ/∂t + ∇·(ρv) = 0 (Continuity)
  • ρ(∂v/∂t + v·∇v) = -∇p + (∇×B)×B/μ₀ (Momentum)
  • ∂B/∂t = ∇×(v×B) (Induction)

Features:

  • Solar wind interaction
  • Earth's dipole field
  • Magnetopause formation
  • Bow shock
